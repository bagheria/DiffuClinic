# DiffuClinic
Diffusion Language Models for Clinical Text

The goal of this project is to fine-tune various diffusion language models on (Dutch) medical text for clinical summarization and systematically compare their performance to autoregressive models of similar size. The focus is on identifying where diffusion-based models outperform or underperform autoregressive approaches in terms of various metrics, such as lexical quality, factual correctness, and efficiency.

1.	Can we efficiently adapt diffusion-based language models for Dutch clinical text summarization?
2.	Do these models produce more factually correct and internally consistent clinical summaries than autoregressive models of similar size?
3.	In which types of clinical content or error modes (e.g. omissions, hallucinations, inconsistencies) do diffusion models outperform or underperform autoregressive models?
